License Number Extraction
By using YOLO algorithm and Vision API, the license plate number is extracted with a very high accuracy in a matter of seconds.
Raspberry Pi
Raspberry Pi acts as the central device in the parking system. Integrated with the IR Proximity Sensor and the PiCam NoIR, and connected to the database, it facilitates the functioning of Internet of Things.
IR Proximity Sensor Switch
Used for the detection of a vehicle as it enters or exits the parking space, the IR Proximity Sensor Switch proves to be highly efficient as it has an adjustable high range of 3-80 centimeters, and it works well even under sunlight or for darker surfaces.
